## Statement

AI systems combining program synthesis and symbolic regression can automatically discover interpretable symbolic models of human and animal cognitive behavior, matching or improving on expert-designed models while producing human-readable expressions.

## Evidence summary

Castro et al. (bioRxiv 2025): program synthesis + symbolic regression discovers symbolic cognitive models from behavioral data in RL task settings. Evaluated against human/animal datasets from cognitive science.

## Conditions and scope

- Controlled RL task behavioral data
- Pre-print (not yet peer reviewed)
- Naturalistic or complex cognitive phenomena not yet evaluated

## Counter-evidence

- Neural cognitive models (Centaur) may have superior predictive accuracy even if less interpretable
- Symbolic expressions may overfit specific task structures

## Open questions

- Do AI-discovered cognitive models reveal genuinely new psychological insights or merely re-derive known models?
- How does this compare to neural cognitive modeling approaches like Centaur?
