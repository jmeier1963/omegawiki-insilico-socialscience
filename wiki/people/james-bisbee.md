## My notes

Political scientist at Vanderbilt working on computational methods and survey methodology. The Political Analysis 2024 paper provides one of the most methodologically rigorous empirical critiques of silicon sampling, documenting the "narrower distribution" problem (LLM synthetic replacements compress within-group heterogeneity). Published in Political Analysis — the top methodology journal for political science — giving it substantial methodological authority.
