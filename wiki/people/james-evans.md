## Recent work

Senior author on the ICML 2025 position paper on LLM social simulations. Known for influential work on how science advances through novel combinations of content and context (Shi & Evans 2020). Co-director of the Knowledge Lab at the University of Chicago and affiliated with the Santa Fe Institute.

## My notes

Highly influential computational social scientist. His work on science of science and knowledge dynamics brings an important epistemological perspective to the LLM simulation debate — particularly the claim that simulations must generalize to out-of-distribution contexts to enable genuine scientific discovery.
